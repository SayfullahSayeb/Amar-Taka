About Amar Taka

Your Personal Finance Companion

Amar Taka is an offline-first finance tracking application designed for students, professionals, and small business users who want complete control over their financial data. Built with privacy and simplicity in mind, it helps you track expenses, manage budgets, and achieve your savings goals.

Unlike traditional finance apps that require cloud storage and internet connectivity, Amar Taka works entirely on your device. Your financial data never leaves your phone or computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What Makes Us Different

  • 100% offline - works without internet
  • Complete privacy - your data stays on your device
  • No ads or tracking - ever
  • Free and open source
  • Works on all devices - phone, tablet, desktop
